How we work

Four principles. No fine print.

A handful of rules we keep returning to whenever we sit down to build something new.

01

On-device first

If a task can be done on your Mac or phone, that's where we do it. Your files, your data, your machine — no detour through a server you didn't ask for.

02

The right tool, per platform

SwiftUI for Mac. React Native and Expo for iOS, with Swift and Kotlin/Java reached for whenever a real platform API is the only honest answer. The point isn't the stack — it's that each app feels fast, quiet, and at home on the system it runs on.

03

Buy once when we can

One-time pricing wherever it makes sense. We don't put a meter on tools that should simply work — and we never charge twice for the same feature.

04

Range over a single bet

One studio, many apps — across utilities, health, lifestyle, productivity, and developer tools. Ideas worth building rarely live in one category, and shipping across them keeps the work honest.
