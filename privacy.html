Privacy

Last updated: 2026-05-20

Coursion Studio builds software that runs on your device whenever possible. This page is the studio-wide summary; each product has its own privacy page with the specifics.

What we collect

On this website: nothing. No analytics, no cookies, no fingerprinting. The page is static HTML served by GitHub Pages.

What our apps collect

By default, our apps do not transmit your data. When a product requires a network call (for example, to query an AI provider you have configured), the request goes directly from your device to that provider. We are not in the middle.
